Getting Started on The Grid

Creating a Site

Welcome to The Grid Beta, we’re excited to have you onboard! Here are a few quick tips to get you up and running.

From your drafts dashboard, click the “Drafts” dropdown menu to reveal the site switcher. Select "Add a Site" to begin the site creation process.

The need for The Grid.

Many sites have come along that have claimed to make site management easier and smarter. So many creatives stay tied down with their actual creative work, that dedicating any time at all to working on a website is met with frustration and limited patience when something doesn't work as they think.
